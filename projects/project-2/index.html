Fall River RE-Creation Golf Tournament Poster
Poster Design
Fall River RE-Creation is a non-profit organiztion, located in Fall River Massachusetts. This posters intended use was for a fundraiser being held at the Acoaxet Country Club in September of 2020. My visual strategy was to create a poster that captured the liesures of golf. To do so I chose a picture with a gradient sunset that would stand out against the overlaying golfer silhouette and text.
