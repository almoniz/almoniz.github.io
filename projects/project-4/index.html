Stung By Royalty
Brand Identity
Stung By Royalty was created for a project I completed in spring of 2020. For this assignment I was to create the brand identity and strategy of a new company that included any type of insect within their products. I chose a bee as my insect that would be used for an all organic lip plumper. My visual strategy was to create an identity that depicted both confidence and beauty.
