Lotta Nieminen Catalog
Publication Design
The Lotta Nieminen Catalog is a school project from fall 2019. In this piece my main goal was to capture the style that Lotta Nieminen uses in her graphic design and illustrations. Her balanced use of postive and negative space prove that "less is more", and the use of basic geometric shapes in Lotta's illustration, show simplicity yet emphasize hidden detail. My visual strategy brings the simplicity and geometric use of Nieminen's work into this catalog by incorporating a simplistic layout with geometirc shapes scattered throughout the catalog.
